The Hyperion is one of the 4 wither blade variants crafted using a wither blade surrounded by 8 L.A.S.R eyes. It is of legendary rarity and offers 260 damage, 150 strength, 350 intelligence, and 30 ferocity at base stats. To use the Hyperion you must have Floor VII completion. Upgrades to this item includes the use of wither essence in the essence shop and 2 unlockable gemstone slots . You can also apply 3 scrolls, Shadow Warp, Implosion. and Wither Shield combined they create Wither Impact You can sell the Hyperion in every way except the bazaar and it can be put in the museum. It also deals 50% more damage to wither mobs and grants 1 damage and 1 intelligence per catacombs level.
